ZENNE ORIGINAL

Hub App

Breda University of Applied Sciences | Promotional Video ★ 9.0

Hub is a platform created by Breda University of Applied Sciences to showcase content made by Creative Business students. For this project, our team was asked to produce two promotional videos, one for social media and one for display screens across campus (without audio). The goal was to highlight student creativity in an engaging and visually clear way.

As both editor and actor, I was responsible for the creative assembly and on-screen delivery of the videos.

  • Editing and color grading both versions for different platforms;
  • Adapting content pacing and visuals to fit silent playback requirements;
  • Acting on camera to represent the student community.
