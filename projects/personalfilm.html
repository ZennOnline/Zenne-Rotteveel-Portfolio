ZENNE ORIGINAL

Project Name

Inholland | Personal Film ★ 9.0

During my time at Inholland University of Applied Sciences in The Hague, I completed several creative and formative projects. This short film was the very first time I explored storytelling through video. While I would approach many aspects differently today, this project represents the moment I discovered my passion for video production and editing, the foundation for everything I’ve done since.

As part of a small two-person team, I was responsible for several aspects of the film’s creation.

  • Concept development and scriptwriting;
  • Acting and production coordination;
  • Filming and video editing.
