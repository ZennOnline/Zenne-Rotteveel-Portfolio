ZENNE ORIGINAL

Culture Shock

Northern Arizona University | LIVE TV production ★ 9.0

One of the most memorable projects during my time at Breda University of Applied Sciences was Culture Shock, a three-week international exchange in the United States. Together with a select group of students, I traveled to Northern Arizona University to produce a live television show from scratch. My main focus was on editing the drag queen segment and designing all on-screen graphics used in the program.

As editor and graphic designer, I handled both visual design and post-production for the live show.

  • Editing the entire drag queen segment from start to finish;
  • Creating all broadcast graphics and visual elements used on-screen;
  • Recording and editing the segment’s audio for final delivery.
