ZENNE ORIGINAL

Veerkracht

Breda University of Applied Sciences | Short Documentary ★ 9.0

One of the projects I’m most proud of from my time at Breda University of Applied Sciences is Veerkracht, a short documentary I co-produced from start to finish with my team. The film tells the story of Karin Trimp, a Dutch woman who rapidly lost most of her sight and is learning to navigate her new world as a visually impaired person. The documentary premiered at Pathé Breda and won the Best Cinematography award.

As editor, I was responsible for shaping the emotional rhythm and tone of the documentary.

  • Editing the complete film from raw footage to final cut;
  • Collaborating closely with the director on narrative flow;
  • Preparing the final version for festival and cinema screening.
