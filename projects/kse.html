The Dutch highschool KSE in Etten-Leur organized an internship fair to introduce students to potential employers and future opportunities. They requested a series of videos: one teaser made with stock footage, one filmed and edited during the event for same-day presentation, and a final aftermovie capturing highlights and student experiences. The project combined fast-paced production with real-time editing challenges.

As editor, I was responsible for all post-production and delivery under tight deadlines.

  • Editing multiple video formats, including a same-day event recap;
  • Coordinating with the filming crew to ensure consistent visual quality;
  • Producing a final aftermovie that reflected the energy and engagement of the event.
