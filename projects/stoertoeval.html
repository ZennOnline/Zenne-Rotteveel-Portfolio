Breda University of Applied Sciences | Music Video ★ 9.0

Stoer Toeval is a Dutch cover band that wanted a promotional music video to showcase at festivals and live events. This project allowed me to combine my love for audio editing with video production. The band provided the tracks they wanted to feature, and I took responsibility for remixing and editing them into one cohesive video performance.

As video and audio editor, I handled both visual storytelling and sound design.

  • Editing the complete promotional video;
  • Remixing and synchronizing multiple songs into one unified track;
  • Adjusting visuals to match musical rhythm and energy.
