January 2019 • Assignment • Amsterdam, NL

ZENNE ORIGINAL

American Book Store

Inholland | Company Film ★ 9.0

During my studies at Inholland University of Applied Sciences in The Hague, I worked on several valuable creative projects. For this particular assignment, I collaborated with the American Book Centre in Amsterdam. It was one of the very first times I got to film content using a professional camera, a learning experience that sparked my passion for production. While my technical skills have improved a lot since then, I have kept this project in my portfolio as a way to show how far I have come.

As Director of Photography, I was responsible for all visual aspects of the project.

  • Operating the camera and framing each shot;
  • Collaborating closely with the team to achieve the desired visual tone;
  • Managing lighting and composition on set.
