ZENNE ORIGINAL

Intercultural Classroom

Breda University of Applied Sciences | Promotional Video ★ 9.0

At Breda University of Applied Sciences, I collaborated on a project for the university itself, focused on intercultural education. The goal was to create informative videos for lecturers on how to effectively teach students from diverse cultural backgrounds within the same classroom. The project combined research-based insight with clear and engaging audiovisual storytelling.

As editor, I was responsible for shaping the educational content into a coherent and engaging video format.

  • Editing footage and structuring the narrative flow;
  • Ensuring clarity and accessibility of information for an academic audience;
  • Collaborating with lecturers to guarantee accuracy and tone.
